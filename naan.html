Naan and Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

Naan

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • Butter for brushing

Paneer Butter Masala

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

Naan

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, salt, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add the yogurt and oil, then mix until the dough comes together. Add water as needed to make a soft dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 2 hours.
  4. Preheat a skillet or tawa on medium-high heat.
  5. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  6. Roll out each ball into an oval shape, about 1/4 inch thick.
  7. Place the naan on the hot skillet and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Brush the hot naan with butter and serve warm.

Paneer Butter Masala

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the paneer cubes and mix gently to coat them with the masala.
  7. Reduce the heat and add the heavy cream. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan.
