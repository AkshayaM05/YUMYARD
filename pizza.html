Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1-2 fresh tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a piece of parchment paper. Brush the dough with olive oil.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese and tomatoes on top of the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Slide the parchment paper with the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and scatter fresh basil leaves on top.
  10. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your Margherita Pizza!
