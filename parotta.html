Parotta and Salna

Ingredients for Parotta

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water, as needed

Ingredients for Salna

  • 500g mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, beans, etc.), chopped
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions for Parotta

  1. In a large bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, vegetable oil, and salt. Gradually add water and knead until you get a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin, flat disc.
  3. Heat a pan or griddle over medium heat. Cook each parotta on both sides until golden brown spots appear.
  4. Repeat with the remaining dough balls.

Instructions for Salna

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add the chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  4. Add the chopped vegetables, turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add water and bring to a boil. Simmer until the vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Add coconut milk and garam masala. Mix well and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with parotta.
